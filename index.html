Meet Our Founder

Abinash Tiwari

Founder & Managing Director

With a deep commitment to health and community well-being, Abinash founded Baundeshwor Udhyog to address the critical need for safe, pure, and accessible drinking water. His visionary leadership has transformed Baundeshwor into a trusted partner for hundreds of hotels, restaurants, and corporate houses across the valley.

Under his guidance, the company has achieved ISO 9001 certification and pioneered eco-friendly packaging solutions, ensuring that every drop delivered not only quenches thirst but also protects our environment.
